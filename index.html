ABOUT US

Park Polytechnic Institute (PPI) is a pioneer polytechnic in Bangladesh for providing Diploma in Engineering, Diploma in Textile engineering under Bangladesh Technical Education Board. Here has Computer, Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Textile Technology. Here has 20 teachers and 500 students. Our institute stand in Sahebpara, Shidderganj, Singboard, Narayanganj 1430.
